# Betachon Estonia

Security is ensured by the Betachon Group, the security service of the Jewish Community of Estonia.

The members of the Group have over 4 years of experience of  outstanding work in ensuring the security of the Community and its  events. The Group has been trained in Israel, Sweden and France, and it  keeps sharing information and best practices with counterpart agencies  in European countries.

The Group members regularly participate in fitness training, leadership workshops and security drills and exercises.

The Group operates on volunteer basis.

Any member of the Community aged over 18 who wants to make a  difference and help the Community may join the Group. We provide an  opportunity of extensive participation in the Jewish Community life,  find new friends, and learn new skills, which will be definitely put to  good use.
